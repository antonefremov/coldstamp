ColdStamp

Your symmetric record of what you signed up for.

ColdStamp does two things at checkout. It warns you about pre-ticked recurring charges, buried renewal terms, and other dark patterns. And it can keep your own record of what the page showed and what you agreed to — stored on your device, never transmitted.

What you need to know

  • Everything stays on your device. No account, no servers.
  • Card numbers and CVVs are redacted before anything is stored.
  • You can export or delete any record from the extension popup at any time.
  • The warning panel runs without storing anything about you.

The full privacy policy covers the details.
